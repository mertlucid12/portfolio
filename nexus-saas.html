FAQ

Common questions.

How long does setup take? +
Most teams are fully set up in under 30 minutes. Connect your data sources, choose a template dashboard, and you're live. No engineering required.
Can I import historical data? +
Yes. We support bulk imports from CSV, SQL databases, and most major SaaS platforms. Historical data is available immediately after import.
Is my data secure? +
Nexus is SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your data, ever.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings at any time. Your data is exportable before you go.

Yes — our Starter plan is free forever. Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
